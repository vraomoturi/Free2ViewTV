List of channels that can be added to Stremium*: 
*Although you can add custom channels in a Stremium Basic plan, a Premium subscription required to be able to record via Cloud DVR.

(Additional channels will be constantly added and this list will be updated regularly.)

How to add channels:
	1. Log into your Stremium Basic account via your PC
	2. Click on CHANNELS (left pane menu)
	3. Click on the + sign at the top of the page.
	4. Type one of the channel names from the list in the CHANNEL NAME field.
	5. Copy the 'm3u8' stream from the list and paste it in the HLS URL field.
	6. "Where is this channel available?" (Leave it as it is.)
	7. "Public" (Leave the toggle switch OFF.)
	8. "Guide Enabled" (This will toggle ON automatically, leave it ON.)
	9. Click on ADD CHANNEL button.
